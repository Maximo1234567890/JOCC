BOOK REVIEW

Percy Jackson, Feb 28, 2024

Percy Jackson is a great book, but not as great as me no cap. If greek mythology were real, I would be like percy jackson, but better, obviously. It is a solid 8.64/10 book, and I reccomend it. The story building is peak, and the characters are likable, but not as likeable as me no cap.

MOVIE REVIEW

The Other Guys, Feb 28, 2024

The other guys is a spectacular comedy about less-appreciated cops. The characters are very relatable, and the writing is funny (not as much as me though). Solid 8.51/10 movie. I recomend you watch it with your family or with your local police station. They will like it, but not as much as they like me.
